ADDING COLLABORATORS TO BTCADP.ORG/COLLABORATE
================================================

PREREQUISITES
- Admin access to Supabase dashboard: https://supabase.com/dashboard
- Project: BTCADP (zjvjptrufipxykwpxfdd.supabase.co)


METHOD 1: VIA THE COLLABORATE PAGE (PREFERRED)
-----------------------------------------------
1. Log in at btcadp.org/collaborate.html with your admin email
2. Scroll to the "Manage Collaborators" panel at the bottom
3. Enter the collaborator's email and a unique handle (public pseudonym)
4. Select their access level:
   - TBB only: can only see The Bitcoin Bridge space
   - Research only: can only see the Research space
   - Both: can see both spaces
5. Select their role:
   - Contributor: post threads, reply, vote (default)
   - Reviewer: all contributor abilities plus change thread status and pin threads
   - Admin: full control including managing other collaborators
6. Click "Invite"
7. Tell the collaborator to visit btcadp.org/collaborate.html and enter their email
8. They will receive a magic link via email, click it, and they are in


METHOD 2: VIA SUPABASE SQL EDITOR
-----------------------------------
1. Go to https://supabase.com/dashboard
2. Open the BTCADP project
3. Click SQL Editor in the left sidebar
4. Run this query (replace values):

   INSERT INTO collaborators (email, handle, display_name, access, role)
   VALUES ('their@email.com', 'their_handle', 'Their Real Name', 'both', 'contributor');

   Access options: 'tbb', 'research', 'both'
   Role options: 'contributor', 'reviewer', 'admin'

5. Tell them to visit btcadp.org/collaborate.html and enter their email


REMOVING A COLLABORATOR
------------------------
Via the collaborate page: click "revoke" next to their name in the admin panel
Via SQL: DELETE FROM collaborators WHERE email = 'their@email.com';


CHANGING A COLLABORATOR'S ROLE
-------------------------------
Via the collaborate page: use the role dropdown next to their name
Via SQL: UPDATE collaborators SET role = 'reviewer' WHERE email = 'their@email.com';


ANONYMITY RULES
----------------
- Contributors see handles only (no real names or emails)
- Reviewers and Admins see handles plus real identity in parentheses
- The handle is what appears on all threads and comments
- Every collaborator MUST have a unique handle


NOTES
------
- Magic link emails come from noreply@mail.app.supabase.io (check spam)
- Magic links expire after 24 hours
- Supabase free tier rate-limits to about 4 magic link emails per hour per address
- The collaborators table seed keeps disappearing: always verify the row exists
  in Table Editor before telling someone to log in
