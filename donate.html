Where It Goes

Every satoshi goes toward keeping the framework open and defensible. There's no token sale, no venture funding, and no equity.

  • ▸Continued research
  • ▸Hosting, infrastructure, and domain costs
  • ▸Establishing the International Digital Monetary Authority (IDMA), a Swiss nonprofit

Other Ways to Help

If you can't donate, the most valuable thing you can do is read the work and tell someone about it. Share The Bitcoin Bridge, challenge the arguments, or apply to contribute directly. Good-faith scrutiny is worth more than money.
