Contribute

Are you a researcher interested in contributing to the project?

The BTCADP framework is an open research project. We're looking for researchers to help verify historical price data, stress-test the monetary instruments, and engage with the architecture on its own terms. All contributions are independently owned by their authors.

A note on identity. The contributor site uses handles to protect participant identity. Reviewers and admins can see your real name, but other contributors will only see your preferred handle. If privacy matters to you, choose a handle that doesn't identify you.
