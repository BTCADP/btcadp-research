How This Works

Live Feeds

Your browser connects directly to exchange WebSocket APIs. No intermediary server processes the data , you see exactly what each exchange reports in real-time.

Computation

The indicative price uses the same aggregation logic as the daily BTCADP: with 5+ feeds, a 25% trimmed mean; with 3–4, the median; with 2, the simple mean. All math runs in your browser.

Limitations

This shows only 4 exchanges (vs 15–40 in the daily calculation), uses last-trade price (not VWAP), and cannot apply trade count or time coverage filters in real-time. It is indicative only.
