International Digital Monetary Authority (IDMA)

Our mission is to safeguard the integrity of Treasury-Backed Digital Currencies by governing the open standard under which they operate, certifying compliance among independent issuers, and ensuring that the framework remains sound, transparent, and resilient as it scales.

Building the institution that governs the TBDC standard. IDMA holds the IP, publishes the specification, certifies compliance, and provides the forum where consortium members coordinate. This space is for developing the organizational framework: from mission and governance to the network monitoring systems that make certification meaningful.
